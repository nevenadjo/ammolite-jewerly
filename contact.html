    Frequently Asked Questions

    • + Do I get a warranty?

    • + How do I use my polishing cloth?
      • To keep your piece looking its best, gently rub the surface with the polishing cloth. Always wash your hands after use to ensure the cleaning agent is removed. Never wash the polishing cloth as it will lose its effectiveness.
    • + How do I determine ring size?
      • While we can never be certain of your size without having physically measured your finger or trying on one of our rings in store, we do have a few handy tricks that may help you determine either your ring sizer or the ring size of a loved one! If you already have a ring with the perfect fit, whether it was gifted or an heirloom piece, grab a ruler and measure the internal diameter.
